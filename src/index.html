🔗 Philosophy

"Drop the damn link, let AI organize it for you!"

This AI-assisted bookmark manager is built for the lazy (smart) way to organize your digital life. Just throw your URLs into "All Bookmarks" and don't worry about folders or tags. Later, when you need something, ask the AI to find exactly what you're looking for based on your queries.

Of course, you can still manually organize things if you're into that sort of masochism... but why? 😉

🛠️ Technical Details

Built with Crawl4AI for intelligent metadata extraction, allowing rich search capabilities across title, description, keywords, and content.

Features:

  • 🤖 AI Auto-Clustering: Semantic organization using TF-IDF & cosine similarity
  • Automatic metadata fetching with rich preview
  • BM25 search with weighted fields
  • Condensed HackerNews-style UI
  • Client-side storage with IndexedDB
